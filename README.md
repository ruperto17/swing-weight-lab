Useful utility web apps for the enthusiast golfer:

1) Calculate the swing-weight of golf clubs using the total weight and balance point, and figure out how much lead tape to add for tunning to a specific swing weight
2) Troubleshoot ball flight patterns based on path, face angle, and gear effect
3) Analyze loft and yardage intervals to ensure no "dead zone" in your bag
4) Track impact, direction, and mental faults during your round. Based on Adam Young's methodology. 

Use the online version here: https://ruperto17.github.io/swing-weight-lab/
